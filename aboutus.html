Now You're Speaking My Language


VIEW PDF




For many organizations, finding the optimal solution is just part of the battle. Getting consensus to implement is another big hurdle to overcome. Even the best projects may receive only lukewarm support if the all-important question of WIIFM – What’s In It For Me? – isn’t addressed. Everyone the new solution will affect should have an understanding of why it is good for them personally and the organization as a whole. This can be challenging since the answer changes by location within the organization, and crafting familiar answers may involve learning some new concepts like ROI.
